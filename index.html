About

Dr. A.P.J Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. He played a major role in India's missile and nuclear programs and was popularly known as the "Missile Man of India".

He inspired millions of students through his speeches, books and vision for a developed India. His dedication, humility and leadership continue to motivate young minds.

Timeline

  • 1931 - Born in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu
  • 1960 - Graduated in Aerospace Engineering
  • 1980 - SLV-3 Satellite Launch Success
  • 1998 - Contributed to Pokhran-II Nuclear Tests
  • 2002 - Became President of India
  • 2015 - Passed away while addressing students

Major Achievements

  • Bharat Ratna
  • Padma Vibhushan
  • Padma Bhushan
  • Developed India's Missile Program
  • Inspired Millions of Students
